The particular aim of our college is to give education that will develop the academic, physical, spiritual and aesthetic side of the students entrusted to our care. Reading habit will be inculcated and extra-curricular activities will be encouraged. The college has a devoted and experienced staff who are fully committed to ensure the individual attention is given to students for their all around development. The college provides education in Five courses at present namely Commerce, Mathematics, Computer Science, Information Technology, English Literature and before long this will be expanded to include all other courses as needed by students. The college Affiliated to the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli and is run strictly in accordance with University Norms, standards and expectations.
